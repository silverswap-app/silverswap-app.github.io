Why swap here

Silver Swap keeps the trade in your wallet

Non-custodial by design

Connect a self-custody wallet, review the Silver Swap route, and sign only when the on-screen quote matches your intent. The app does not need a deposit account to show a route.

Built around ETH to Silver routes

Start with Ethereum assets such as ETH and review the Silver side of the trade before confirmation. Stable assets including USDC may be available where the app supports them.

Clear route, time and cost checks

Silver Swap shows the live quote, route path, network fee context and confirmation flow in one place, so you can compare the trade before your wallet broadcasts it.

How it works

Use Silver Swap in three steps

1

Connect your wallet

Open the app from the CTA, choose your wallet, and keep custody of your funds while the interface prepares the route.

2

Choose ETH and Silver

Select the asset you want to pay with and the Silver asset you want to receive. The route preview stays number-free here; the live quote is inside the app.

3

Review and sign

Check output, timing, network cost, approvals and slippage controls in the wallet flow before authorizing the swap.

FAQ

Common Silver Swap questions

01

What is Silver Swap?

Silver Swap is a non-custodial DeFi swap app for trading from a connected wallet into a silver-focused crypto asset. It is built for users who want a clear route review before signing.

02

Which chains and assets are supported?

The preview highlights ETH to Silver, and the app may also show other supported assets when available. For the current network list, use the supported chains page.

03

How long does a Silver Swap take and what does it cost?

Timing and cost depend on the selected route, wallet confirmation and network conditions. Silver Swap shows the live quote and network fee context in the app before you sign.

04

How should I check safety before swapping?

Confirm the token, route, wallet prompt, spending approval and destination asset. Tools for token approvals can help you review allowances after using DeFi apps.
